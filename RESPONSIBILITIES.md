# Security Responsibilities

## Technical Department (Ilie Lucian – Manager)

- Network security architecture (firewall, VLAN, VPN)
- Security monitoring & detection (Wazuh, DarkGhost, Snort, Zenarmor)
- Backup & disaster recovery procedures
- Access control (SSH keys, WireGuard, 2FA enforcement)
- Incident technical response
- Security updates (kernel, packages, unattended-upgrades)
- Compliance support for IT controls (auditd, logging)
- Change management (testing in lab before production)

## IT Department (reports to Technical Department Manager)

- User account management (Zoho, Odoo, WireGuard)
- Workstation security (laptops, updates, antivirus)
- End-user support & troubleshooting
- Asset inventory (hardware, software)
- Employee onboarding/offboarding (technical part)

## Accounting / HR Department

- GDPR Art. 30 – Records of Processing Activities
- Data retention policies (legal/compliance)
- Privacy notices & employee consent
- Data breach notification to authorities
- Contracts with data processors (DPA)
- Employee GDPR training (administrative part)
