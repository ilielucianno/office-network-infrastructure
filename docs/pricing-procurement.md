## Budget Notes

- All prices are **approximate** and may vary depending on vendor, shipping, and current exchange rates.
- Total budget: **under €1000**
- All components were purchased **new** with **official invoices** (VAT included)
- The server (Mini PC) was chosen for low power consumption and sufficient performance for 12 concurrent users
- Cables were purchased locally to avoid shipping delays
